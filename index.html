How to Evaluate: Peer Reviews
This metric measures the level of external validation the certificate required.

Low Score (1-3): You received an automated "100%" on a multiple-choice quiz with no human oversight.

Medium Score (4-7): Your final project was graded by a teaching assistant (TA) or went 
through a formal peer-review system where other students had to approve your work.

High Score (8-10): Your work was graded directly by a professor or an industry expert, 
or it was part of a published research paper or a major open-source contribution 
that was vetted by the community.
Indicate the complexity level of related projects 
How to Evaluate: Project Complexity
This metric measures the effort, depth, and rigor of the certification.

Low Score (1-3): You watched a 2-hour webinar or completed a simple, 
self-paced "Intro to Gen AI" video series with no final test.

Medium Score (4-7): It was a multi-week course (e.g., from Coursera or a university)
 that included quizzes, assignments, and a final, auto-graded exam.

High Score (8-10): This was an intensive, multi-month program or a
university-level course that required you to build and submit a unique capstone project
